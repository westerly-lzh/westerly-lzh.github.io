Who We Are

PEAPROS Technology Services (FZE) is an IT service company focused on the energy industry, established in 2017.

Headquartered in Dubai, PEAPROS serves its customers in Asia, Afraic and Australia.

PEAPROS acquired the IT Service Business of Dialog Group Berhad in 2021, took over all the on-going contracts.

PEAPROS provides customers with industry leading expertises and solutions to assist them to achieve outstanding business objectives.
