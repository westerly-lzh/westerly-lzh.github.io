Who We Are

PEAPROS Technology Services (FZE) is an IT service company focused on the energy industry, established in 2017.

Headquartered in Dubai, PEAPROS serves its customers in Asia, Afraic and Australia.

PEAPROS merged with the IT Service Business of Dialog Group Berhad in 2021, and took over all the on-going contracts.

PEAPROS use the power of technology to deliver mission critical IT services across the Enterprise Technology Stack to drive business impact in the Energy Industry.PEAPROS provides customers with industry leading expertises and solutions to assist them to achieve outstanding business objectives.

PEAPROS is committed to be the closest Digital partner of its customers.
